Our Story

How Dabelo Began

Dabelo Café was founded in 2025 at 9, Emmanuel Street, Irepodun, Shangisha-Magodo, Lagos — with one simple goal: to make healthy, delicious food accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Started by nutrition enthusiast Omotola Sodipo, Dabelo is more than a café — it's a movement. We believe good health starts with what we eat, and that everyone — from busy parents to young professionals — deserves fresh, natural meals without compromise.
