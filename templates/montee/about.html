Our Story

How Motee Began

Motee Cakes began with a simple belief: every celebration deserves a cake as unique and meaningful as the moment itself. From intimate gatherings to grand occasions, we pour artistry, care, and the finest ingredients into every creation.

Based in Lagos, our studio is a haven for creativity — where custom designs meet meticulous baking to bring your vision to life. We specialise in cakes that taste as extraordinary as they look.
