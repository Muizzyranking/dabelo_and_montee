Our Philosophy

Where Wellness Meets Celebration

At Dabelo & Motee, we started with a simple conviction: Lagos deserves better. Better food. Better ingredients. Better care in every box, every bottle, every slice. Dabelo Café brings you vibrant, health-forward refreshments — cold-pressed juices packed with real nutrition, antioxidant-rich salad bowls, and clean meal prep you can feel good about.

Motee Cakes is where artistry meets occasion. Every cake is handcrafted to order using only premium, carefully sourced ingredients — designed with you in mind, baked with intention, and delivered as a centrepiece worthy of your most important moments.
