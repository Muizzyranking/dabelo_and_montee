She kept asking herself —

"Why is eating well
so hard in Lagos?"

As a nutrition enthusiast and busy mother, Omotola Sodipo saw the same struggle everywhere — parents reaching for processed snacks because fresh food felt out of reach, out of time, out of reach on a budget.

She decided to change that. Not with a big idea on paper, but with a kitchen, real ingredients, and a stubborn refusal to cut corners.

She decided to change that.

01
Dabelo Café

Healthy food
you can trust.

In 2025 Omotola opened Dabelo Café — a small kitchen with a big promise: fresh, honest food should be affordable and available every day.

Every juice is cold-pressed that morning. Every bowl uses seasonal produce from farmers she knows personally. No preservatives. No shortcuts.

The weekly meal prep service quickly became a favourite among busy parents who wanted their children to eat well even on rushed mornings.

"I wanted Dabelo to feel like a promise — that what you're putting in your body is genuinely good for you."
— Omotola Sodipo, Founder
